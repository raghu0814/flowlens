FlowLens

Detect recurring SAP pricing condition gaps before they impact revenue

FlowLens is a SAP-complementary diagnostic tool that analyzes extracted pricing data to identify recurring pricing condition issues and revenue risk — without changing SAP.

Designed for large SAP landscapes in manufacturing and regulated industries.

Discuss SAP pricing issues

Why SAP pricing issues keep repeating

  • Pricing condition records expire or go missing over time
  • Issues are detected late during order processing or billing
  • SAP reports show transactions, not recurring patterns
  • Business impact remains unclear until escalations or audits

How FlowLens helps

FlowLens works as a read-only diagnostic layer. It identifies systemic pricing condition gaps, highlights repeating patterns, and estimates revenue risk — so teams know where to act.

  • No SAP configuration changes
  • No system integration required
  • Business-friendly diagnostic insights
  • SAP-safe and audit-ready

How it works

  1. Export pricing outcome data from SAP (Excel / CSV)
  2. Upload data into FlowLens
  3. FlowLens identifies recurring pricing condition issues
  4. Business and SAP teams fix issues directly in SAP

Typical insights highlight repeat pricing failures, impacted order volume, and indicative revenue risk — helping teams prioritize fixes.

FlowLens does not execute or change SAP pricing. It only diagnoses.
